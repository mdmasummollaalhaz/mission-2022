Global Community

Globalization is the gradual process of the world shrinking as people of different nations, cultures and religions know more about one another. Western ideas and modes of living are seeping into the fabric of the world. The American baseball cap is now worn from Tahiti to Timbuktu. McDonald’s is ubiquitous. Everyone on earth is forced to adjust to information technology. No nation can modernize without becoming part of the global information system. Existing cultures, traditions and institutions thus become threatened, even disappearing.
